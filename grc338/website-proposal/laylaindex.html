Introduction

Athenian Grill

At Athenian Grill, we offer a variety of menu items to suit the most diverse apppetites and preferences. We serve a hybrid of Greek and American food blended together with love. Choose from many light and healthy Greek selections, or just go for the double pastrami burger! Complement your meal with wines and brews from our eclectic list featuring local producers and Greek imports.

Target audience

People who are in the mood for greek food and a waterfront view in Suisun city will use this website.

Our goal at Athenian Grill is to provide excelent food and service for those looking to relax on the waterfront.
